What is Cassava?
A blockchain infrastructure that connects the value chain of content creation through an incentive consensus mechanism that benefits consumers, content producers and platforms in the ecosystem
Creators A new way of monetizing their content protecting their content copyright
Users User's specific positive contributions can be quantified and rewarded
Advertisers Better exposure opportunities, supported by transparent data
